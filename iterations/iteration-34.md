### 1. Soriano Extends to Laoac — Jovito Quevedo Soriano
- **Jovito Quevedo Soriano** = Barangay Captain of Anis, Laoac
- Confirms the Soriano principalía network extends from San Carlos → Laoac
- This PREDATES the 2025 election of Faustino Q. Soriano Jr. as Laoac Municipal Councilor
- "Quevedo" middle name = mother's maiden name, a Spanish surname suggesting principalía background
- Soriano is now confirmed in: San Carlos (2 captains), Manaoag, Laoac (captain + councilor)

### 2. Rebugio Dynasty Dominates Laoac
- **TWO Rebugio barangay captains**: Analyn Tabilin Rebugio (Caaringayan) + Elton Boy Serra Rebugio (Casampagaan)
- **PLUS** Christopher Barcena Rebugio as Kagawad of Poblacion
- THREE Rebugio officials across different barangays = dominant Laoac political family
- Previously noted in iteration 25 that Rebugio was a dominant surname (2 councilors + 2 captains)

### 3. Perez in Laoac — Rodolfo Avenida Perez
- **Rodolfo Avenida Perez** = Captain of Cabilaoan West
- Perez = same surname as Speaker Eugenio PADLAN Perez of San Carlos
- The Perez surname extending to Laoac further connects to the San Carlos-Laoac principalía network

### 4. Eleseo Eslao Jr — Succeeded His Assassinated Father
- **Eleseo Bergonia Eslao Jr** = Captain of Talogtog
- His father Eleseo Eslao Sr was **assassinated July 2, 2021** by motorcycle-riding gunmen

- The "Jr" strongly suggests the son took over the captaincy after his father's murder
- Two barangay captains from family-connected municipalities killed within 2 weeks of each other

### 5. Tabayoyong Dynasty Confirmed at Barangay Level
