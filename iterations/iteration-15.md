- Co-founded PDPSTA in September 1968 at a meeting in Urdaneta
- Served on Ad Interim Board alongside: Mariano O. Hidalgo (President), Arturo M. Caronongan (VP), Carlos G. Beltran, Anselmo G. Untalan, Pompouso G. Rivera, and others
- **Elected first PDPSTA President on June 23, 1969**
- PDPSTA survived intense rivalry with existing teacher associations, went through court litigation (1969-1971)
- Eventually became PPSTMBA Inc. (Pangasinan Public School Teachers Mutual Aid Benefits Association)
- Organization founded December 17, 1968 (constitutional convention), SEC registered March 28, 1969
