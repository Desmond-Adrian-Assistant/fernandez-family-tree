## NARA Philippine Army/Guerrilla Records — Confirmed Process (NARA.gov)

From official NARA page (archives.gov/personnel-records-center/military-personnel/philippine-army-records):

### What's Covered
- Philippine Commonwealth Army (created Dec 21, 1935)
- USAFFE forces (July 26, 1941 – June 30, 1947)
- **Recognized Philippine Guerrilla forces** (units under Gen. MacArthur's GHQ approval)
- Covers: naturalization purposes, veterans benefits, government agency applications, verification of awards

### Request Process
- **Primary method:** SF-180 (Request Pertaining to Military Records) — mail to NARA
- **Address:** National Personnel Records Center, 1 Archives Drive, St. Louis, MO 63138
- **Family members** (descendants) can request with: death certificate + proof of relationship (birth/marriage certificates showing lineage)
- **For Mariano Fernandez:** Raul Fernandez (grandson) can submit with: Mariano's death certificate + birth certificates showing Mariano → Raul lineage

### Important Limitation
Collection only provides **individual service verification**, NOT unit history or battle details.
