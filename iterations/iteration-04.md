### 2. Dagupan Cathedral (Metropolitan Cathedral of St. John the Evangelist)
- Located on **Burgos Street, Dagupan City**
- Cathedral of the Archdiocese of Lingayen-Dagupan
- Records date back to the late 1590s/early 1610s (Augustinian era)
- FamilySearch has microfilmed records covering **1615-1982**
- This is the parish where Bienvenido and Aurea were almost certainly baptized and married
