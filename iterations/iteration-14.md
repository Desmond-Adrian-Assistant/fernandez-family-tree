### 🔥 MAJOR: Barangay Don Justo Abalos — Umingan, Pangasinan
- A **barangay named after an Abalos patriarch** exists in **Umingan municipality**, Pangasinan
- Formerly called "Caroan," renamed to honor **Don Justo Abalos** — a local leader important enough to have a barangay renamed in his honor
- Population: 1,198 (2020 Census)
- The "Don" honorific indicates principalía status (landed gentry/political leadership)
- Umingan is in eastern Pangasinan (~40 km from San Carlos)
- **Don Justo Abalos Elementary School** being established there (House Bill 4036)
- Confirms Abalos as a **principalía surname across multiple Pangasinan municipalities**
