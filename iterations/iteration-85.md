### 5. Pampanga Historical Context
- Angeles City was named after its founder Don Angel Pantaleon de Miranda (1796)
- City charter approved by President Diosdado Macapagal (common Filipino first name — also Janice's father's name: "Diosdados")
- Clark Air Base (3 miles west of Angeles City) employed 70,000+ Filipinos
- The base economy created a working-class/technical class that could emigrate post-1991 closure
- This strongly supports the Cervantes family's Chicago immigration pathway via Clark Base employment
