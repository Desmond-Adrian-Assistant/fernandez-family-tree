### Finding 3: FERCOLLA Clan — The Dominant Fernandez Family of Dagupan
Detailed the FERCOLLA (Fernandez-Cóquia-Llamas) clan from blog and news sources:

**Lineage of the Political Fernandez Dynasty:**
- **Don Antonio Llamas Fernandez** (Municipal President 1911-1915) married **Capistrana Bernal**
  - Children: Aurelio, Jose, Amado, Paz, Miguel, **Angel**
  - **Jose Bernal Fernandez** — publisher of Tunong Magazine (1920s-30s)
  - **Angel B. Fernandez** — became Mayor of Dagupan, then Congressman (2nd District, Pangasinan). Made San Carlos a city (1965).
- **Alipio Fernandez Sr.** — Mayor when Dagupan became a chartered city (June 20, 1947). Married **Fredesvinda Fernandez**
  - Children: Lourdes, Corazon, Carmelo, Antonio, **Alipio Jr.**, Fredesvinda, Asterio, Angela, Alejandro, Marian
  - **Alipio "Al" Fernandez Jr.** — Mayor 1992-2001, later Bureau of Immigration Commissioner. Died 2018 age 75.
    - Children: **Alfie** (Alipio Serafin), **Alvin**, **Anna** Fernandez-Cunanan
    - Grandson: Carlos Alipio Serafin Fernandez
- **Armando Cabal Fernandez** — Vice Mayor, co-author of the FERCOLLA book. Grandson of Don Antonio.
- **Belen Fernandez** (current Mayor) — **no relation** to the Alipio branch. Father was "Jimmy Fernandez"

**Connection to Cóquia Family:**
- **Don Modesto Hortaleza Cóquia** (Municipal President 1907-1909) married **Genoveva Fernandez**
  - Children: Calixto, Juan, Julieta, Esperanza, Francisca, Macaria
  - Grandson: **Dr. Ignacio Coquia de Guzman** (regional health director)
- **Alvin Coquia** is a current Dagupan City Councilor

**Current Dagupan City Council (2022-present) showing FERCOLLA influence:**
- Michael Fernandez (Councilor)
- Maria Lourdes Fernandez (Councilor)
- Alipio Serafin Fernandez (Councilor)
- Alvin Coquia (Councilor)
