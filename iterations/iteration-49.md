### Villanueva Surname Analysis
- **277,730 bearers in Philippines** — 10th most common surname nationally
- Spanish origin: "villa" + "nueva" = "new estate/town"
- Too common to narrow geographically (unlike Daquigan with 1,781 bearers)
- Listed as a Pangasinan surname on Wiktionary
- Most prevalent in Philippines globally (725th most common surname worldwide)
- **Implication:** Without specific parish records, the Villanueva line is hard to trace — Marta could be from any town in Pangasinan/Tarlac
