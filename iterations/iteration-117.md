This reveals **TWO SEPARATE systems** holding Mariano Fernandez WWII records:

#### System 1: AFP NRD/OTAG (Philippine-Side Records)
- **NRD** = National Records Division
- **OTAG** = Office of The Adjutant General, Armed Forces of the Philippines
- Location: Camp General Emilio Aguinaldo, Quezon City, Philippines
- Contains: Philippine Army's own enlistment/service records for WWII-era soldiers and recognized guerrillas
- **This is the OFFICIAL Philippine government record — separate from NARA**
- If Mariano was recognized as a USAFFE soldier or guerrilla fighter, his record EXISTS HERE
- Access: Written request to NRD, OTAG AFP — family members can request with death certificate + proof of relationship

#### System 2: NARA NPRC (US-Side Records)
- **Address:** 1 Archives Drive, St. Louis, MO 63138 (confirmed by PVAO's own guidance!)
