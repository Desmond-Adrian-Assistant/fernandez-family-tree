### NARA Philippine Army/Guerrilla Records — Request Process Documented

From the National Archives (archives.gov/personnel-records-center/military-personnel/philippine-army-records):

**What it contains:**
- Records of individuals who served in the Philippine Commonwealth Army (USAFFE) and recognized guerrilla forces during WWII
- Personal histories, diaries, unit histories, medical/death reports, guerrilla unit activities

**How to request (for Mariano Fernandez):**
1. **Who can request:** Veterans, family members (next of kin), or with signed authorization
2. **Method:** eVetRecs (online) or SF-180 form (mail)
3. **Address:** National Personnel Records Center, Military Personnel Records, 1 Archives Drive, St. Louis, MO 63138
4. **Requirements for next-of-kin:** Copy of death certificate + proof of relationship (birth certificate)
5. **Adrian's father Raul** could submit as grandson of Mariano Fernandez with appropriate documentation

**Actionable recommendation for Adrian:** Ask Raul if Mariano served in the military/guerrillas, then request records via SF-180 form.
