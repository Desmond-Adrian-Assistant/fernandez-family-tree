## Key Findings

### 🔑 Bacarra, Ilocos Norte — Official Municipal History Confirmed
- **Founded officially as a municipality: 1778** (by Manuel Paras, per Tripod history source)
- **Population (2024):** 32,734 people — small municipality
- **Location:** 9 km from provincial capital Laoag; bounded by Pasuquin (N), Vintar (E), Laoag (S), South China Sea (W)
- **Parish records:** Municipality founded 1778 → parish established around same time
- **Pre-1778:** Area was under jurisdiction of LAOAG parish (or Pasuquin) — records would be in the parent parish

### Implication for Bacarro Research
- Benita Bacarro's ancestors were baptized in Bacarra, Ilocos Norte
