## Key Finding: Circulo Capampangan Chicago — Potential Research Resource

From Pinoy News Magazine March 2026 obituaries, Conrado "Jun" Manankil Jr. (d. Dec 2025, age 86) is described as **founder of Circulo Capampangan in 1970** — "a social organization dedicated to uniting sons and daughters of Pampanga and Tarlac in the Midwest."

- **Founded 1970** — same era as Diosdados Cervantes would have been in Chicago
- **Oldest Kapampangan organization in North America** per Inquirer 2015
- **Members from Angeles City and surrounding Pampanga/Tarlac communities**
- Hosted first national Kapampangan convention in US/Canada in August 2015
- Also: Hector Luat (died Las Vegas) was a member — confirms it's a real organization still active
- If Diosdados Cervantes and Natividad Cabrera immigrated to Chicago in the 1970s-80s from Angeles City, they almost certainly were members or known to Circulo Capampangan
