### 🔥 Conchita Rizo Tamondong — Chicago Tamondong CONFIRMED

From Pinoy News Magazine September 2025 issue, obituary of **Purificacion Rizo Gamalinda** (age 90):
- "Surviving sister: **Conchita (Wilfredo) Tamondong**"
- Purificacion's husband: late Cesar A. Gamalinda
- Other surviving siblings: Natalia (Horacio) De Ocera, Pacita (Rene) Panlilio, Antonietta (late Warren, Sr.) Callanta
- Also mentioned: "late Tomas, Jr. (Frida) Rizo, Iluminada (late Jesse) Goyena and **Conchita (Wilfredo) Tamondong**"
- Wake: Colonial-Wojciechowski Funeral Home (the main Filipino funeral home in Chicago)
- Memorial Visitation: Lakeside Church of Chicago

**Significance:** Conchita Rizo married a Wilfredo Tamondong. Both are in the Chicago area. This adds to the documented pattern:
- Jessica Tamondong Rhee (State Farm agent, N. Ave, Chicago)

- Now: Conchita Rizo Tamondong (sister of Purificacion Rizo Gamalinda)

The "Rizo" surname is not directly connected to Adrian's family, but Wilfredo Tamondong represents another confirmed Tamondong in the Chicago Filipino community.
