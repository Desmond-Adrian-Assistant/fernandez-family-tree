## 🔥 MAJOR DISCOVERY: Bienvenido A. Tamondong — Government Official in Laoac

From the official Laoac municipal government website (laoac.gov.ph/about-us/):

> "Mr. Bienvenido A. Tamondong, incumbent Municipal Local Government Operations Officer of DILG-Laoac, was appointed as 'caretaker' of the municipality from December 2, 1987 to January 1, 1988"

### Significance:
- **Name "Bienvenido"** — same first name as Adrian's grandfather Bienvenido Fernandez (1933-1994)
- **Surname "Tamondong"** — one of Adrian's maternal ancestral lines (through Victoria/Victoriana Daquigan's mother Carlina Tamondong's parents: Jose Abalos married Carlina Tamondong)
- Wait — re-checking the family tree: Actually, Juan Tamondong + Juana Paglingayen are great-great-great-grandparents. The Tamondong surname enters via Jose Abalos marrying Carlina Tamondong.
- **Middle initial "A."** — could stand for many names
- **Location: Laoac** — a municipality in Pangasinan that borders San Carlos City (one of the closest cities)
- **DILG official** — Department of Interior and Local Government career professional
- This person may be a relative from the Tamondong line who stayed in Pangasinan (while Adrian's family emigrated to Chicago)

### Could this be Adrian's grandfather?
- Adrian's grandfather Bienvenido Fernandez (surname Fernandez, not Tamondong) died in Chicago in 1994
- This Bienvenido A. Tamondong was working in DILG-Laoac in 1987-1988
- Different surnames (Fernandez vs Tamondong), so this is NOT the same person
- But having the same unusual first name + the Tamondong surname in the same small area strongly suggests family connection
- "Bienvenido" is not a common Filipino first name — the clustering in the same Pangasinan area (San Carlos + Laoac) is noteworthy
