### 1990 Luzon Earthquake — Confirmed Dagupan Devastation
- 7.7 intensity, July 16, 1990
- 48 dead, 14 missing, 24 injured in Dagupan alone
- Vice Mayor **Alipio Fernandez Jr.** (FERCOLLA) chaired rehabilitation task force
- St. John Cathedral (Dagupan) suffered major damage (tower collapsed, killed tricycle drivers)
- 80% of buildings on **Perez Blvd and A.B. Fernandez Ave** collapsed
- Magsaysay Bridge totally collapsed
- **If Bienvenido's family was still in Pangasinan in 1990, they would have experienced this earthquake**
- Bienvenido died in Chicago in 1994 — the earthquake may have accelerated emigration
