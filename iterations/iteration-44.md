### 2. Abando Surname Analysis (Forebears.io)

- **Total bearers:** 4,212 worldwide

- **Rank:** 110,363rd most common surname globally — very rare
- **Philippines distribution:** 21% in **Ilocos Region**, 18% in NCR, 17% in Calabarzon

- **Origin:** Not listed on Forebears, but "Abando" is also a district in **Bilbao, Basque Country, Spain** — same Basque region as the Abalos surname origin (Ábalos, La Rioja)
- **Phonetically similar:** Obando (129,048 bearers) — a much more common Filipino surname

### 3. Significance of Abando

- The Abando surname's concentration in Ilocos (21%) is consistent with the family's broader Ilocano network (Daquigan = Ilocos, Tamondong = 47% Ilocos, Rosario = 44% Ilocos)
- The Basque/Spanish origin possibility parallels the Abalos surname's Basque origin (La Rioja)
- Abando as a district of Bilbao: the old Abando neighborhood was historically Basque-speaking and was absorbed into Bilbao in 1890
- The surname may connect to early Spanish colonial settlers, similar to other pre-Catálogo Spanish surnames in the family
