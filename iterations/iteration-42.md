### Mariano Fernandez WWII — Assessment

Mariano Fernandez (b. ~1905, San Carlos/Gerona) would NOT appear in US military records because:
1. He never emigrated to the US (he died in the Philippines)
2. He fought in the Philippine Commonwealth Army or guerrilla forces, NOT the US military
3. Philippine guerrilla records (NARA RG 407) are **NOT digitized on Ancestry**

### Where Mariano's WWII Records Would Be

1. **NARA Record Group 407, Entry 1087** — Philippine Army/guerrilla personnel files
   - Files 185-1 to 185-30: Army of the Agno (local Pangasinan force)
   - File 308-23: 1st Pangasinan Regiment, PTMD/ECLGA (Col. Ramsey's network)
   - Physical records only — National Archives, College Park, MD

3. **Filipino Veterans Recognition and Education Project (FilVetREP)** — registry not publicly searchable
