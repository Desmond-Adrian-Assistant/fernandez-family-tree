### Holy Family Parish — Barangay Tandoc, San Carlos City

**Confirmed details:**
- **Parish:** Holy Family Parish
- **Archdiocese:** Lingayen-Dagupan
- **Vicariate:** Saint Dominic (same as Minor Basilica of St. Dominic — the main San Carlos cathedral)
- **Parish Priest:** Fr. Douglas C. Nicolas
- **Established:** 2007
- **Location:** Barangay Tandoc, San Carlos City, Pangasinan 2420
- **Feast Day:** Sunday within the Octave of Christmas

**Significance:** Barangay Tandoc is where:
1. **Ramil Macalanda** is barangay captain (the Macalanda family that intermarried with Tamondongs)
2. **Anastacio Tandoc Abando** — US SS record holder whose mother's maiden name was Tandoc (from Tandoc barangay)

The Holy Family Parish opened in 2007 — too recent for historical records. Pre-2007 Tandoc barangay parishioners would have worshipped at the Minor Basilica of St. Dominic (the main cathedral), and their records would be in that church's archives, which are part of FamilySearch collection #1646454.

**Key insight:** All Tamondong, Abalos, and Rosario families in San Carlos — including those from Tandoc barangay — would have their pre-2007 baptism/marriage/death records at the Minor Basilica of St. Dominic. This confirms collection #1646454 is the single most important FamilySearch resource for Adrian's entire paternal and maternal side.
