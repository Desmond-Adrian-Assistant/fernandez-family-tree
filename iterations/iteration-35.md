### 🔥 Eclar Dynasty: The Husband-Wife Mayor/VP Swap

**Engr. Eloy Castillo Eclar** — BS Civil Engineering (St. Louis University, Baguio). Municipal Engineer of Gerona from ~1990. NPC member. Elected Mayor:
- 2019-2022 (1st term)
- 2022-2025 (2nd term, won again)
- Hit 3-term limit → **wife ran for Mayor in 2025**

**May Batenga Eclar, Ph.D.** — Won Mayor 2025 (30,836 vs Nonoy Go's 22,842). Eloy simultaneously ran for Vice Mayor and won (39,858 vs Atty. Manny Gragasin's 13,170).

**"Doc Gerry Batenga"** ran for councilor (12,406 votes, lost) — **Batenga is May Eclar's maiden name**, suggesting another family dynasty member.

This is the classic Filipino political dynasty maneuver: term-limited incumbent has spouse run for the seat while the incumbent drops to vice mayor.

### 🔥 Ortiz-Arciaga-Simeon-Acob Intermarriage Network (bonarae.net)

The extensive **Ortiz family tree** on bonarae.net (maintained by family member, sourced from parish records back to 1841) documents Gerona's founding family intermarriages:

**Key connection:** Eulalia Ortiz Novesteras (1914-1992) married **Pedro Arciaga Simeon** (1910-1982), son of **Segundo Simeon** and **Nicolasa Arciaga**.
- This confirms **Arciaga** as a prominent old Gerona family
- The Simeon-Arciaga marriage parallels the Fernandez-Capinding marriage pattern

**Acob family:** Carolina **Acob** Simeon (1905-1989) married Buenaventura Ortiz (1895-1985). The Acob surname appears again with **Alfred Acob Jr.** winning councilor in 2025 — multi-generational political family.

**California migration:** Multiple Ortiz and Arciaga family members emigrated to California (San Mateo County, Daly City, Los Angeles) — same pattern as Fernandez emigration to Chicago.
